About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Chemical Engineering at Eskisehir Osmangazi University, Turkey, working under Prof. Dr. Macid Nurbaş. My research focuses on PEEK (polyetheretherketone) polymerization digital twins and multi-filler composite modeling.

Research Projects

1. PEEK Digital Twin Development

Developed a digital twin for PEEK step-growth polymerization using Method of Moments and neural networks, achieving 99.35% prediction accuracy.

2. Multi-Filler Composite Modeling

Created validated micromechanical models for PEEK composites with carbon nanotubes, graphene, carbon fibers, and glass fibers using Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 samples).

3. Wastewater Treatment

Research on activated carbon from walnut shells for uranium adsorption, conducted at the Nuclear Research Center of Draria (CRND), Algeria.
